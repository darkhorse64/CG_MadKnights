read color:word(1)

gameloop
loop 8 read line:word(8)
read moveCount:int
loop moveCount read move:word(4)
write random

STATEMENT
Move your knight and block your opponent!

INPUT
color: your piece color ("r", "g" or "b")
line: horizontal row
moveCount: number of legal moves
move: a legal move

OUTPUT
e.g. e3 (move piece to e3) or random
